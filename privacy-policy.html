Privacy Policy

Rapid Recovery: Sports Therapy and Performance LLC

Effective Date: [Insert Date]

Rapid Recovery: Sports Therapy and Performance LLC ("we," "our," or "us"), including its DBA Rapid Recovery: Elite Training Center, is committed to protecting the privacy of our members, guests, and website visitors. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard personal information obtained through our website, facility, memberships, services, and digital platforms.

By accessing our website, entering our facility, purchasing a membership or day pass, or using our services, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

Personal Information You Provide

This includes information you voluntarily provide when you sign up for a membership, purchase a day pass, complete waivers or forms, or communicate with us, such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Mailing address
  • Date of birth
  • Emergency contact information
  • Payment and billing information
  • Account login credentials

Fitness and Facility-Related Information (Non-Medical)

We may collect non-medical fitness and facility usage information, including:

  • Training preferences or self-reported goals
  • Attendance history and service participation
  • Access control records (key fob, QR code, or app-based entry)

This information is collected solely for operational, safety, and service purposes and is not medical information or protected health information under HIPAA.

Website and Technical Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and usage activity
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

Communications

We may collect and retain records of communications with us, including emails, text messages, form submissions, and customer support inquiries.

Audio and Video Recording; Use of Likeness

Rapid Recovery: Sports Therapy and Performance LLC operates a private training and performance facility and may record audio, video, and photographic content within the facility, including but not limited to training areas, recovery areas, and event spaces.

By entering the facility, purchasing a membership or day pass, or participating in any activities or services, you acknowledge and agree that you may be recorded, photographed, or filmed.

All audio, video, and photographic recordings captured within the facility are the exclusive property of Rapid Recovery: Sports Therapy and Performance LLC. Such recordings may be used, in whole or in part, for legitimate business purposes, including but not limited to:

  • Marketing and promotional materials
  • Advertising and social media content
  • Educational and informational content
  • Website and digital media publications

Any such use will be made in good faith and will not be used to knowingly defame, misrepresent, or disparage any individual.

Consent to Recording and Waiver Acknowledgment

As a condition of membership, day pass access, or participation in services, all members and guests are required to sign a separate waiver and release agreement acknowledging and consenting to audio and video recording, as well as the use of their name, image, likeness, and voice as described above.

If you do not wish to be recorded, you should not enter the facility or participate in activities where recording may occur.

Physical Therapy Privacy Carve-Out

Audio, video, or photographic recording conducted within Rapid Recovery: Sports Therapy and Performance LLC's gym, training, and performance areas does not apply to physical therapy services, clinical evaluations, or treatment areas where protected health information may be discussed or provided.

Recording of any physical therapy session, clinical interaction, or treatment-related activity will occur only with the patient's separate, written authorization and in compliance with applicable healthcare privacy laws, including HIPAA.

This Privacy Policy applies solely to gym, training, recovery, and non-clinical areas of the facility. Physical therapy services are governed by a separate Notice of Privacy Practices.

How We Use Your Information

We may use personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, manage, and administer memberships, services, and facility access
  • To process payments and transactions
  • To communicate regarding scheduling, billing, updates, policies, and facility notices
  • To improve our services, website, and overall member experience
  • To send marketing, promotional, or informational communications (with opt-out options where required)
  • To enforce our waivers, policies, and terms of use
  • To comply with applicable legal and regulatory requirements

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal information.

We may share information with:

  • Third-party service providers (including membership management software, payment processors, email/SMS platforms, website hosting providers) solely to perform services on our behalf
  • Legal or governmental authorities when required by law, subpoena, court order, or to protect our legal rights and safety
  • A successor entity in the event of a merger, acquisition, restructuring, or sale of assets

All third-party providers are required to safeguard information and use it only for authorized purposes.

Security and Surveillance

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect personal information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

For safety and security purposes, the facility utilizes video surveillance systems. Surveillance footage is used for security, safety, operational, and business purposes and is not shared except as permitted by law.

Your Choices and Rights

You may:

  • Request access to or correction of your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing emails or text messages
  • Request deletion of your account, subject to legal and operational requirements

Requests may be submitted by contacting us using the information below.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on our website with an updated effective date. Continued use of our website or services constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
